Ceramics by Rose

A Passion for Ceramic Creation

Rose has been creating ceramic pieces since 2007, starting with school classes to officially building her own home studio.

Her home studio allows her to work on whatever pieces she would like to at her own convenience, which truly shows her passion for the art.

As a student with a lot going on, Rose still makes time to get spinning some clay on the wheel and firing it in the kiln if she ever wants a new coffee mug or soup bowl.

The Options are Endless

Rose has created small cups and bowls all the way up to a 2.5 foot tall Buddha sculpture. Her natural talent has opened a lot of possibilities up for her.

As much as ceramic work is her main passion, Rose has a plethera of artistic skills involving painting, drawing, landscaping, etc,.
