About Ceramics by Rose

Rose Masi, located in a small town south of Albany, NY, enjoys spending her free time in her home studio developing ceramic art.

Rose's first college degree is an Associate Degree in Fine Arts. From a young age, Rose was always heavily invested in creating works of art. She found her niche in the ceramics world, and has always kept up with it!
